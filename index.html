The Monkey Center

Some monkeys live on the ground, while others live in trees. Different monkey species eat a variety of foods, such as fruit, insects, flowers, leaves and reptiles. Most monkeys have tails. Groups of monkeys are known as a 'tribe', 'troop' or 'mission'.

The Shark Tank


Sharks are ancient animals. They've been on earth for at least 420 million years. Sharks, unlike most fish, don't have bones, but cartilage, which is a soft, sinewy substance. They have several rows of teeth and might have as many as 3,000 teeth in their mouths at one time.

Zebra

Zebras are really cool animals and they look pretty neat too with all those amazing stripes. Bet you didn’t know this. Zebras can run seriously fast. In fact, they can run up to 65 miles (105km) per hour.
